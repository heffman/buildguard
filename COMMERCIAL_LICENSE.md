# Commercial Licensing

`buildguard` is free for personal and other non-commercial use.

Commercial use requires a separate paid commercial license. That license can be
delivered as a separate commercial bundle or agreement, but the software itself
does not perform activation, unlock, phone-home, or license-file checks.

The commercial license should cover at least:

- internal company use
- use in commercial CI/CD systems
- use by consultants or agencies for client work
- redistribution rights only if you explicitly want to grant them

Keep the commercial license text and packaging workflow in a private companion
repository.
